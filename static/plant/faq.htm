1.       How do I check moisture of plant?

・         Check moisture under the 3 water drops icon.

・         ‘1’ tell you that your soil is Dry

・         ‘2’ tell you that your soil is Normal

・         ‘3’ tell you that your soil is Wet

2.       How do I check temperature of surrounding?

・         Check temperature under thermometer icon

3.       How do I check Water level of water tank?

・         Check it under water bottle icon, 0 for no water, 1 for have water inside the tank

4.       Why my machine made a ‘dee’ sound?

・         This is because the water tank is empty, refill the water!

5.       How to refill water?

・         Rotating the cap to open the water tank

・         Fill in water until full

・         Fill in every day

6.       How to use this?

・         Put soil sensor into soil of plant.

・         Connect to router(modem)

・         Type http://webplant.local/ on your browser address bar with device (can be computer, mobile phone and others) in same network

・         You now can monitor your machine from your browser.

7.       Why webpage no responding?

・         There might be some problem, turn off the power and open it in next 4 seconds.

8.       I cannot connect to http://webplant.local/ on my pc or mobile phone

・         Check wheater address is correct

・         Install Apple Bonjour Print Service for windows at http://support.apple.com/kb/DL999

・         Windows 10 user had to find webplant’s ip address on local network manually.

・         Sometimes it wont work on some devices, please do find ‘webplant’ ip in router inferface, or either using Fing apps on Play Store and App Store to find out ip address.

・         IP will be in format of 192.168.x.x

・         Browse http://192.168.x.x

9.       Can I use machine without Internet?

・         Yes, you can, but you would not access it from public, just only accessable within your house network.
